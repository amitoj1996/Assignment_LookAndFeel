About this Website:


This website contains information about police stations present in Seattle, Maryland and Chicago. Users can get the police station name, phone number, address, and coordinates for all the police stations for their respective cities. Google maps integration will be added in the future to display coordinates on the map. In addition to this users can access cases solved by policemen and also access description of cases.
